wunderdogs

Every year around 4 million dogs enter the U.S. shelter system. When it comes to finding a home, some dogs have an especially hard time. Deemed 'less adoptable,' these are the breeds and types of dogs rescue groups have the greatest difficulty placing. For a chunky chihuahua, an old Shepard, or a dog with three legs, the wait to be adopted is often 4X as long - if they find a home at all.

Wunderdogs lets you search shelters near you for dog types that don’t always catch the eye of potential adopters.
