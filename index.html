Wunderdogs

Shelter dogs in general often struggle to find homes, and some types have it particularly bad. For a chunky chihuahua, an old Shepard, or a dog with three legs, the wait to be adopeted is often 4X as long, if they find a home at all.

Wunderdogs lets you search shelters near you for dog types that don’t always catch the eye of potential adopters.
